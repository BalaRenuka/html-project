Overview


Set in Hyderabad, 25 km from Nehru Zoological Park, Clapping Leaves Resort offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a restaurant. The property is around 28 km from Chowmahalla Palace, 28 km from Mecca Masjid and 28 km from Charminar. The accommodation features room service and a 24-hour front desk for guests. Guests at the resort can enjoy an à la carte breakfast. You can play mini-golf at Clapping Leaves Resort. Golkonda Fort is 31 km from the accommodation, while AP State Archaeology Museum is 31 km away. Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is 8 km from the property.Booking.com
